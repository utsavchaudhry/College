How to Use
  • Model: Choose a mesh from the Model dropdown. Pattern scale is auto-sized to the model’s bounding radius.
  • Lighting:
    • Light A (angle, radius, height) orbits the model (transformed to camera space per frame).
    • Light B is fixed in camera space (height slider).
  • Camera: Use ← ↑ → ↓ to orbit. Use Z / X to zoom in/out.
